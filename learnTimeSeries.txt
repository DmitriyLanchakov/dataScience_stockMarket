what is 

	time series

		sequence of data points made:

			1) over continuos time interval
			2) out of successive measurements across that interval
			3) using equal spacing between every two consecutive measurements
			4) with each time unit having at most one data point

		examples include:
			ocean tides
			counts of sunspots
			daily closing value of the Dow Jones Industrial Average
